About Us

Amaks Global Luxe Projects Limited is a fully registered real estate company with a primary focus on the sale of verified and genuine lands and properties across strategic locations in Nigeria.

Sales is at the core of what we do. We guide our clients through smart property decisions—whether for investment or personal use—by offering carefully vetted properties with clear documentation and strong value potential.

We also provide property rental assistance strictly on request, limited to Abuja and Owerri locations, ensuring reliable and well-screened options.

Beyond property sales, we are backed by a team of professional engineers responsible for building construction and site supervision, ensuring quality execution and structural integrity. We also deliver bespoke interior design services, transforming spaces into functional and elegant environments.

At Amaks Global Luxe Projects Limited, we don’t just sell properties — we help you secure value, confidence, and peace of mind.
